Alex Patri is an Argentine musician installed in Mexico more than 4 years ago. Through from different experiences musicals, Alex has a career spanning many years touring as a singer and guitarist. Based on the needs that along the way became fundamental for the artist, Mara Vs the fire dragon was born. trying to find the perfect space with the right technique so that the experience is totally satisfactory. Mara Vs el dragon of fire is located in the center of Colonia Santa Catarina in Coyoacán, CDMX.
